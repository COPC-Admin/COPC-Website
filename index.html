Welcome to
City of Prayer Embassy!

At City of Prayer Embassy, our journey is deeply rooted in the transformative power of prayer, a vital conversation consistently guided by the Holy Spirit and made possible through Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior. Through our collective communion with Him, we've discovered a profound hunger for God's presence and heavenly perspective, allowing divine insights to shape our path as a community.

It is in these moments of prayer, in reliance on Jesus' finished work, that we find clarity, receive divine enablement, and witness true transformation, not just in our individual lives but in the broader impact of God's will unfolding through our very existence as the City of Prayer. Prayer isn't merely what we do; it's who we are – a continuous pursuit of God's heart and purpose, centered on Christ, for our city and beyond.

Our Leadership

Prophet Jules Dessin

Prophet Jules Dessin is a dynamic Christian minister celebrated for his fervent and biblically grounded teachings, deeply emphasizing the transformative power of consistent prayer, especially dedicated night prayers. Through his ministry, empowered by the Holy Spirit, he passionately guides believers, equipping them with the spiritual understanding and practical tools necessary to effectively engage in spiritual warfare. This enables them to overcome formidable challenges, receive divine intervention through Jesus Christ, and ultimately step into the fullness of their spiritual destiny, inspiring a renewed dedication to prayer and a deeper walk with God.

First Lady Jessica Dessin

First Lady Jessica Dessin, wife of Prophet Jules Dessin, is a powerful and spiritually astute leader. She is possessed of a profound prayer life, navigating spiritual realms and waging "stratospheric" warfare to advance God's kingdom. Driven by a "birthing spirit," she is a woman of divine blueprints and strategy, understanding spiritual legalities to conquer territories and establish God's culture. With a "head-crushing anointing," she fearlessly contends with spiritual darkness, serving as a living altar for God and embodying a transformed, authoritative presence that brings access and deliverance to generations.
